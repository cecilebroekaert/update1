Pet Body

An animation I made with only the use of gif's. The song pet body from the artist Miya Follick was for me interpreted as a song that touches on upon the issues around social axiety, mental health and unhealthy relationships. I wanted to make a mix of images and techniques because of ups and downs of emotions in the song and the difficulty of making images for issues that are quit abstract.
2018, Mixed media on paper.
